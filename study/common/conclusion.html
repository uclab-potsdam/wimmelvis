In need for systematic change

Taking part in the recycling process of where you live, is the one of the easiest and most climate neutral actions one can do to help reduce their waste and impact that this provokes. While using primary resources increases the amount of CO2 that is emitted into the environment, recycling plays a substantial role in contributing to reducing emissions since it generates renewable sources. Our goal with this article is to not only advocate for recycling, but also to bring awareness that this process has to be done correctly, by knowing materials and how they can properly be processed to generate new resources.
