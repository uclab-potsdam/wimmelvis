Pizza Box

Its made out of carton. BUT: If it is stained with grease from the pizza then it can't be recycled as paper and should go into the Hausmüll.

Tetrapack

Its made out of around seven differrent materials like aluminium, plastic and paper. Most of it can be recycled and goes into the Wertstofftonne.

Shipping Box

The different components like tape and stickers need to be separated from the carton box in order for it to go into the Papiertonne.

Envelopes

As long as the main component is paper it can be disposed of in the Papiertonne. Paper clips, staples and small amounts of plastic can get sorted out in the recycling process of paper.

Receipt

The thermal paper has a special coating which hinders the recycling process. This is why they should go into the Hausmüll.

Plastic Bag

Not even a bio-degredable plastic bag can go in the Biotonne, because it composts slower then other bio-waste. You should dispose of it in the yellow bin Wertstofftonne.

Cooking Foil

It belongs into the yellow bin Wertstofftonne, as well as packaging like food containers made out of aluminium.

Medication

It is still important to throw them away correctly, or else they can harm the enviroment. There are designated recycling centers for disposal and some pharmacies take them back as well.

Broken Mirror

It is important to never throw mirrors into a glass-recyling collection station, they can ruin whole charges of recyclable glas. Instead you can put them in the Hausmüll.

Yogurt Cups

The cup and even the lid can go into the Wertstofftonne. BUT: You should always separate lid and cup and also not stack cups together. This makes the separation with a scanner and recycling easier. They also don't need to be perfectly clean, just free of most food leftovers.

Broken Cable

They often are made out of a metal like copper or aluminium and have a isolation layer of plastic. The different layers get separated in an extensive separation process, before they can be reused. BUT: To guarantee that the valuable metals get recycled, cables should go into the Wertstofftonne rather then the Hausmüll.

Broken Plate

Porcelain is a organic connection of different kinds of rocks, but they don't get recycled because it would cost to much energy and the raw materials are available in large quantities. BUT: It is important to never throw porcelain into a glass-recyling collection station, they can ruin whole charges of recyclable glas. Instead you can put them in the Hausmüll.

Broken Toaster

Only if you bring it back to a store selling electrical appliance or bring it to one of the recycling centers. They don't belong in the Hausmüll. Components like copper, aluminium and zinc, that are found in most electronics can be extracted and recycled without any loss in quality and the plastic, e.g. from covers, is grinded done into granules and can be reused as well.

Batteries

They contain different metals which can be separated and reused. BUT: In order to be recycled they can't be thrown away in the Hausmüll. You can bring them back to a store, any store that sells them is legally required to take them back.

Chain Of Lights

While old light bulbs and halogen lamps can be thrown away into the Hausmüll, chains of light are considered electronis and should be brought to a recycling center. They can contain electronical components like capacitors and LEDs, which can be reused or recycled. That is the same for chains of light with small light bulbs.

Dead Plant

You can dispose of the dead plant in your compost or in the Biotonne and reuse the soil for another plant or just spread it out outside.

Coffee Filter

It goes into the Biotonne, which than is used for biogas plants and what is left is compost for agriculture.

Bike Tire

Not if they go into the Hausmüll. For recycling you can bring it back to a bike shop or bring it to one of the recycling centers.

Plastic Bottle

Most get recycled via the Pfandsystem, they are returnable bottles. They get grinded into granulate, the PET is reused for packaging and the other recycled materials can be used for fabric fibres or foil. But this needs a lot of energy, so it is always better to use reusable plastic or glas bottles. If they aren't returnable bottles, they belong into the Wertstofftonne.

Printer Ink

It is still important to throw them away correctly, or else they can harm the enviroment. There are recycling center and stores take them back as well. As many toner cartridges are produced with oil it is even better to get them refilled at specialised stores.

Tea Bag

It goes into the brown bin Biotonne, which than is used for biogas plants and what is left is compost for agriculture.

Old Pan

Pans are made out of metals, either aluminium, stainless steel, iron or copper, and can in any case be melted and reused. The plastic or wooden handels can be recycled as well, only the teflon coating has a too low melting point to be recycled. Simply dispose of them in the yellow bin Wertstofftonne.

Glass Bottle

To ensure its recycling you should return it to the store if it is a returnable bottle and if not bring it to a glass-recyling collection station. When recycled they need to be sorted by color to ensure the right glass color for the reuse. In case you have a different colored bottle and no multi colored bin (Buntglascontainer) is there, you can put e.g. blue and red colored glas bottles into the green glass containern. Green glass can contain up to 15% of different colored glass and still be recycled into the right color.

Old Clothing

Especially clothing, shoes and bed linen in good condition are a great donation. They get donated to people in need by organisations like Kleiderkammer der Berliner Stadtmissionand Berliner Kältehilfeand almost every city has a similar organisation. If not, old clothing collection points(Altkleidercontainer) are another great option, you should just check for FairWertung-label. Unusable clothing can be disposed in the (Hausmüll).

Coffee To Go Cup

To hold liquid, they have a plastic coating and contain 7% plastic. That makes them so hard to recycle that their production is banned in the EU from July 2021. If you find one anyways, then they belong into the Wertstofftonne.

CD

As they get less popular, it is important to recycle them correctly. They contain valuable plastics, mostly polycarbonate, which makes their correct recycling important to help the enviroment and reduce the oil use. You should bring them to a recycling center, the polycarbonate can there be extracted and reused in items like lenses, suit cases, helmets, syringes and phone and tablet casings.
